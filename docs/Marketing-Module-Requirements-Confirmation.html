4. Compliance & Deliverability (Important)

Since you mentioned wanting emails to land in the inbox and not spam, here are things I need to set up regardless of which platform I choose:

RequirementWhat It IsStatus
SPF Record DNS record that tells email providers "Mailgun is allowed to send email on behalf of cqvs.com.au" Needs Check
DKIM Signing Cryptographic signature that proves the email hasn't been tampered with Needs Check
DMARC Policy DNS record that tells providers how to handle emails that fail SPF/DKIM checks Needs Check
Unsubscribe Link Australian Spam Act 2003 requires every marketing email to have a working unsubscribe mechanism Must Build
Sender Identity Marketing emails must clearly identify the sender (CQVS) and include a physical address Must Include
Legal note: Under the Australian Spam Act 2003, sending marketing emails or SMS without consent or without an unsubscribe option can result in fines. I will build unsubscribe handling and consent tracking into the system regardless of which platform is used.
