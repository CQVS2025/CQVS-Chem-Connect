4 Compliance & Deliverability (Important)

Since you mentioned wanting emails to land in the inbox and not spam, here are things I need to set up regardless of which platform I choose:

RequirementWhat It IsStatus
SPF RecordDNS record that tells email providers "Mailgun is allowed to send email on behalf of cqvs.com.au"Needs Check
DKIM SigningCryptographic signature that proves the email hasn't been tampered withNeeds Check
DMARC PolicyDNS record that tells providers how to handle emails that fail SPF/DKIM checksNeeds Check
Unsubscribe LinkAustralian Spam Act 2003 requires every marketing email to have a working unsubscribe mechanismMust Build
Sender IdentityMarketing emails must clearly identify the sender (CQVS) and include a physical addressMust Include
Legal note: Under the Australian Spam Act 2003, sending marketing emails or SMS without consent or without an unsubscribe option can result in fines. I will build unsubscribe handling and consent tracking into the system regardless of which platform is used.
